Our Story

Where every cup
tells a story

Born in the lanes of Mallapur, Hyderabadi Amruttulya is built on one belief — that a great cup of tea can ground you. We use traditional jaggery, whole spices, and milk sourced fresh daily. No shortcuts, no substitutes.

From a humble stall to a beloved neighbourhood café, we carry the warmth of Hyderabad in every sip we serve.
