The Beginning

Hyderabadi Amruttulya was born from a simple conviction: that the best tea in the world isn't found in luxury hotels — it's brewed in the hands of someone who's made it every single morning for decades.

We opened our doors in 2020 in Mallapur, Secunderabad, with nothing but a handcrafted recipe, an iron kadai, and an unshakeable belief in the power of jaggery chai.

The Craft

Every cup at Hyderabadi Amruttulya is brewed to order. We use natural block jaggery, whole spices ground fresh each morning, and full-fat milk from a local dairy. No premixes. No shortcuts.

The Kulhad Tea — our signature — is served in hand-fired clay cups that add an earthy, mineral note you simply can't replicate in glass or plastic.

The Community

Mallapur is more than our address — it's our family. We've watched kids grow up, seen friendships form over morning chai, and witnessed countless early mornings before work turn into moments of real peace.

That's what we make. Not just tea. A reason to pause.

Looking Forward

We're growing — new recipes, new faces, and soon, new locations. But one thing won't change: every cup will be made the same way it always has been, with patience and pride.

Because some things shouldn't be scaled. They should be savoured.

What we stand for

Our Values

Natural Ingredients

No artificial flavours, no premixes. We use raw jaggery, whole spices, and fresh milk — every single day, without exception.

Brewed Fresh

Every order is made from scratch. We don't keep batches sitting around. Your tea is your tea — made the moment you ask for it.

Community First

Affordable prices, warm service, and a space where everyone belongs. This is a neighbourhood café that stays true to the neighbourhood.
